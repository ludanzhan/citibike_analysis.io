Citibike Analysis

Background

As one of the popular commute tools, the bike is especially well-known for its effects on environmentally friendly, daily exercise, and money savings. With the emerging public bicycle sharing system, Citi Bike has expanded the usage and popularity of bike riding. Over the past eight years, Citi Bike has successfully grown average ridership numbers to above 100,000.

Project Objective

This project aims to provide a deep insight into the Citi Bike history including the user demographic distribution (age, gender, and user type), time-series analysis (overall bike usage growth and the influence of covid), and the station's geographic distribution. All data used in this project can be found in the Citi Bike public data site
